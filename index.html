Brown Ales

Reddish or brownish in color, these beers that are pretty mild in flavor. Flavor association with brown ales tend to offer hints of caramel, toffee, or coffee.

Pale Ales

Lighter-colored beers that have a sharper, more bitter flavor. Included in this family of ales are Amber, American Pale (APA), Blonde, Irish Red, and India Pale Ale (IPA).

Porters

A dark style of beer that tends to offer flavor notes that include chocolate, coffee, or roasted grains.

Stouts

Dark, rich beer that is similar to a Porter. Some people would refer to these beers as being “thick”, or “creamy” compared to other beers and tend to be darker and “thicker” than porters.
